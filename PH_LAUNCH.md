## First Comment (Founder Story)
Hey all —

I built PaidUp Collections after watching a friend spend 3-4 hours every Friday manually following up on overdue invoices. Polite emails that got ignored. Awkward phone calls. Revenue just sitting there, uncollected.

The data backs this up — automated follow-up sequences recover 30-40% more than manual chasing. The key is timing and tone. Friendly nudge on day 1. Gentle reminder on day 3. Firm follow-up on day 7. Escalation notice on day 14. Each email has a proven template, and the sequence runs completely on autopilot.

What PaidUp Collections does:
- Import invoices from QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe, or FreshBooks
- 6-step smart email sequence tuned for maximum recovery
- Real-time dashboard — see exactly who's overdue and what's been recovered
- Full recovery stats: monthly reports, aging breakdowns

Three plans: $29/$79/$199 based on invoice volume. 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

I built this to be fully self-serve — no sales calls, no onboarding meetings. Import your invoices and let the sequences run. Check the dashboard when you want to feel good about recovered money.

Happy to answer any questions!

— Scy
